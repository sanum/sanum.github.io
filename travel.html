Accommodation

Delegates are responsible for their own arrangements.

There are numerous bed-and-breakfast places (Guesthouses) near UJ (Melville, Auckland Park, and surrounds ). Here are few suggestions.

  • Twickenham Guest House (Linda 082 782 4628)
  • Melville Gap Guest House (Marius Neuhoff marius@gaphospitality.co.za)
  • 7thstreet (Rudi info@7thstreet.co.za)
  • Lucky Bean Guesthouse (Conway info@luckybeanguesthouse.co.za)
Another possibility is to check at Airbnb or Lekke Slaap.

Safety

Walking alone in Johannesburg, off campus is not recommended. The university surrounds are normally safe and well populated with students but precautions should always be taken. Uber is available and daily commutes from Melville to UJ should be R20 - R40 and take less than 10 minutes.
