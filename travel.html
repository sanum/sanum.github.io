Travel by Uber/shuttle/car (from CPT)

Stellenbosch is situated about 50km east of Cape Town International Airport (CPT).

These days Uber is probably the easiest way to get to Stellenbosch from CPT airport. Typically costs are between R400-R500.

If travelling via car from Cape Town airport, the N2 highway is usually considered the safest route.

Accommodation

Delegates are responsible for their own arrangements. Accommodation in Stellenbosch is usually at a premium in March/April so early bookings are advisable. One thing to keep in mind when you make reservations is that Stellenbosch does not have a public transport system. If you do not have your own transport, you should plan to stay within walking distance of the conference venue (see the maps above) or be prepared to use an Uber.

There are numerous bed-and-breakfast places (Guesthouses) in and around Stellenbosch. Here are few suggestions, all within walking distance from the conference venue: Bonne Esperance or Roosenwijn Guest House. For student accommodtion, check out Stellenbosch Apartments or Just Joey. Another possibility is to check at Airbnb or Lekke Slaap.

Safety

Although Stellenbosch has always been a quiet and relatively safe town, it is not entirely free of crime. It therefore a good idea to take normal safety precautions at night time, such as travelling in groups and avoiding dark alleys.

Travel in the Western Cape is usually safe, but it may be a good idea to discuss any travel plans with one of the organisers. As a general rule public transport is not recommended. If you plan to do sightseeing, renting a car at the airport is a good option. Alternatively there are several rental agencies who operate in Stellenbosch. These days, Uber has a solid presence in Stellenbosch and Cape Town.
