Registration

Registration for SANUM2026 is now open. Please enter your registration details here.

Shortly after registering you will receive a confirmation email indicating your total registration fee and the relevant banking details.

After registering, we encourage you to book accommodation as soon as possible to avoid disappointment.

Once you have registered, please submit your talk title and abstract here.

Please note that registration is not considered complete until proof of payment of the registration fee is received.

SANUM2026 registration Fees

Early bird registration (before 27 Feb 2026)R1750
Late registrationR2250
Student* registrationR750
Additional conference dinner ticketsR650

Registration fee covers conference and workshop attendance, tea/coffee, lunch, welcome reception, and the conference dinner.

*To be eligible for reduced registration, students may be asked to provide proof of their student status from their institution.

Student/early-career travel funding

The conference organisers have limited travel support* available for students and early-career researchers** from South Africa.

  • Up to R5000 is available for 10 attendees to cover travel and accommodation expense, plus the cost of registration.
  • The recipient must attend the congress in-person and present either a poster or a presentation.
  • Funding requests will be reviewed on a first-come first-served based with priority given to attendees from Historically Disadvantaged Institutions.
  • Note that expenses will be reimbursed after the conference.
  • Please complete this form in order to apply for this funding. Proof of eligibility may be required.
  • Contact the conference organisers for additional information.

* The organisers thank the DSI-NRF CoE-MaSS for this funding. ** Here early-career means having submitted their PhD with the last five years.
