Stedent/early-career travel funding

The conference organisers have limited travel support available for students and early-career researchers* **.

  • Up to R5000 is available for 10 attendees to cover travel and accommodation expense, plus the cost of registration.
  • The recipient must attend the congress in-person and present either a poster or a presentation.
  • Funding will be awarded on a first-come first-served based and expenses will be reimbursed after the conference.
  • Please complete this form in order to apply for this funding. Proof of eligibility may be required.

* The organisers thank the DSI-NRF CoE-MaSS for this funding. ** Here early-career means having submitted their PhD with the last five years.
