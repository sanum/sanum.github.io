SANUM2024

The 2024 symposium will be a 2.5 day event hosted by Stellenbosch University. The symposium will start after lunch on Wednesday 03 April and conclude in the afternoon of Friday 05 April. A list of confirmed plenary speakers can be found here.

In addition to the symposium, there will be a 1.5 day pre-conference workshop on the Julia programming language, with an emphasis on its DifferentialEquations.jl and SciML packages, organised by Sheehan Olver of Imperial College London.

See the conference programme for further details.
