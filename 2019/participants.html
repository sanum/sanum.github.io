Student Competition

Student presentations will be judged and prizes awarded for the best Masters and best Doctoral presentation. Make sure you indicate you are a student when you register for the conference. 

Student Funding

Funding is available to cover a student's conference registration (not travel and accommodation). 

Email this completed form together with 1) proof of degree registration, 2) proof of full-time registered student (not your proof of registration – a letter from your HOD confirming you do not work more than  12 hours a week) and 3) a copy of your South African ID or relevant passport to inger.fabris-rotelli@up.ac.za with the following subject line Funding for PG students SANUM 2019. Application forms which are incomplete will not be considered.

Closing date: 15 February 2019
